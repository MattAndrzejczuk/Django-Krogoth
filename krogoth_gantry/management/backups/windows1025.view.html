Windows 10 requires manual configuration to play Total Annihilation

Older versions of Windows would usually take care of this automatically, but it's no problem and very easy to resolve on Windows 10.

1. Open Control Panel

2. Go to "Apps & Features"

3. Click on "Programs and Features" on the right side

4. Select "Turn Windows features on or off"

5. Under "Legacy Components" check the DirectPlay checkbox then click OK.
