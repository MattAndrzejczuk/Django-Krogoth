Total Annihilation Lazarus RC 1.0 July 10th, 2017 The most resilient Total Annihilation mod editor in the galaxy.

TA Lazarus is a new and powerful web based application which allows you to build highly customizable mods for Total Annihilation. Initially with version 1.0 RC, only Vanilla Total Annihilation will be fully supported, but Lazarus is close to being able to coexist with other already massive TA mods such as The Pack and TAESC.

One of the biggest features of Total Annihilation Lazarus is automatic conflict crushing. Finally, you will have the ability to add a vast amount of units to your Total Annihilation installation without worrying about unit command card conflicts as seen below.
