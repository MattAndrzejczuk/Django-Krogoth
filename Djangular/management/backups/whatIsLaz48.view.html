What is Lazarus

Total Annihilation Lazarus is a new web based mod builder and conflict crusher for the 1997 classic Total Annihilation released by Cavedog entertainment.

Arm Prime will serve as a new social network that'll allow members to upload and share TA modding resources that can be easily added to any Lazarus mod project within the community.

Total Annihilation itself was not only ground breaking, but built to age very well. Dispite the fact that there has not been a single patch or update made to Total Annihilation since 1999, the game fully supports 4K resolution and has aged very well.
