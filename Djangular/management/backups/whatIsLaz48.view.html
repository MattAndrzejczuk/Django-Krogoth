What is Lazarus

Total Annihilation Lazarus is a new web based mod builder and conflict crusher for the 1997 classic Total Annihilation released by Cavedog entertainment.

Arm Prime will serve as a new social network that'll allow members to upload and share TA modding resources that can be easily added to any Lazarus mod project within the community.

Total Annihilation itself was not only ground breaking, but built to age very well. Dispite the fact that there has not been a single patch or update made to Total Annihilation since 1999, the game fully supports 4K resolution and has aged very well.

How it works
Upload
Analyze
Modify
Publish
Upload any of the HPI/UFO files used by Total Annihilation and our upload repository will automatically extract and analyze the contents of the game data you provide.
We've developed a powerful dependency scanner that will break up and organize the extracted HPI contents and save them to our database. FBI and TDF files are converted into a more modern JSON form and finally stored in PostgreSQL.
Change the values of unit properties easily using our web interface and share them with other members of the modding community.
Once you're mod is ready, you'll simply click publish and Lazarus will automatically compile all of your custom values back into a fresh .UFO file ready to download and be shared with the community.
